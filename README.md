Welcome to **Butterf1y**, a pleasing, energetic and effective platform built with HTML, CSS and JavaScript.
**The goal:** To empower students struggling with social anxiety to discover and attend events they might otherwise avoid. We encourage you to step outisde your bubble, push your limits, and meet new people who share your interests and your challenges. Start your next adventure today.
# Description ðŸ“–
Upon entering the site, students are greeted by the eye-pleasing homepage and intuitive navigation bar featuring Community, Events and About Us sections. Users can read event reviews firsthand to ensure the specific gathering aligns with their comfort levels and interests before commiting.
# Features ðŸŒŸ
* **Home Page:** Offers insights from previous users of the site, how it helped them overcome their anxiety, shows our goal and offers the possibility of new users to create an account or to log in.
* **Community Page:** Reviews of recent events, left by users of the Butterf1y.
* **Events Page:** Showcasing upcoming events by the users' filters and interests, offering event bundles, with discounts for the first-timers. This is where users can buy tickets.
* **Sign-up Page:** Enter your credentials, hobbies and to select which type of user you are (student or event planner) and create your account.
* **About Us Page:** Presentation of the team behind the Butterf1y project and the thoughts behind the creation of this idea.
* **Profile Page:** A presentation of your public profile to the rest of the users.
* **Footer:** The website's contact information.
